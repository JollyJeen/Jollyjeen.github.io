"Anime Slasher: Chop the zombies" is an exciting 3D video game that will immerse you in a world plagued by a zombie epidemic, full of danger and dynamic battles! A battle for survival in this creepy city awaits you. As a hero armed with a sword, you will have to fight zombies throughout 30 exciting levels. Each level will bring you new challenges and unique types of monsters. Be ready for non-stop battles and incredible challenges that will test your skills. After completing the main levels, even more opportunities await you in the endless mode, where you can continue to fight endless waves of zombies, trying to set new survival records. Character customization, allows you to be unique in this apocalyptic world. You will be able to choose different cosmetic options, equipment and clothing.
