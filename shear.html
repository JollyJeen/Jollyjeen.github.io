Noob's Barbershop - Shear the Sheep!

Welcome to the exciting world of farming, where you have to help little and brave Noob to cope with a flock of colorful sheep on his farm. Only you can help him in this important task. This game requires you not only dexterity but also attention. You have to be fast to have time to shear the sheep in accordance with Noob's instructions. With each sheep the game gets harder and harder, how far will you go? Set new records, improve your skills and compete with other players.
